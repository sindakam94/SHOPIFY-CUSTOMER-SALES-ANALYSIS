# 🛍️ Shopify Sales and Customer Funnel Report Dashboard

An interactive and analytical Microsoft Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Shopify sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, retention trends, product performance, payment preferences, and regional sales distribution across the United States.

---

# 📌 Description 

The Shopify Sales and Customer Funnel Report is a dynamic Power BI dashboard built to help businesses monitor e-commerce performance through key operational and customer-focused metrics.

### The dashboard provides actionable insights into:
- Net sales performance
- Customer retention behavior
- Product category contribution
- Payment method trends
- Geographic sales concentration
- Order quantity analysis
- Customer funnel metrics


---

# 🛠️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- 📊 **Microsoft Power BI** – Dashboard development and data visualization
- 📂 **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
- 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – KPI calculations and dynamic measures
- 🌍 **Bing Maps Integration** – Geographic and regional sales visualization

# 📂 Data Source

### Source
Simulated Shopify e-commerce transactional dataset.


# ✨ Features & Highlights

## 📉 Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of transactional and behavioral data, making it difficult to quickly identify:

- Which products generate the most revenue
- Which customer segments are most valuable
- What payment methods dominate purchases
- Which regions drive the highest sales
- How effective customer retention is
- What purchasing patterns occur throughout the day

Without centralized analytics, identifying growth opportunities and operational bottlenecks becomes challenging.

---

# 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard was designed to:

- Track overall sales performance
- Monitor customer retention and repeat behavior
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Analyze customer and order trends by hour
- Evaluate payment method preferences
- Visualize regional sales performance
- Support strategic e-commerce decision-making

# 📈 Business Impact & Insights

## 💡 Customer Retention Insights
- Repeat customer rate of **46.02%** indicates strong customer loyalty.
- Purchase frequency of **1.68** suggests customers frequently return for additional purchases.

---

## 💡 Revenue Optimization
- Running Shoes generate the highest sales volume and customer engagement.
- Businesses can prioritize inventory and promotions around high-performing categories.

---

## 💡 Payment Strategy Optimization
- Shopify Payments is the preferred checkout method.
- Businesses can optimize checkout experiences around dominant payment preferences.

---

## 💡 Geographic Expansion Opportunities
- Strong sales concentration in major US cities indicates high-performing regional markets.
- Underperforming regions can be targeted for localized campaigns.
